• Improved Gale Shapely Algoritmn

The Gale-Shapley algorithm, a classic solution to the stable marriage problem, can be enhanced using heuristics. One approach involves incorporating weighted preferences, allowing individuals to express the strength of their preferences. Introducing tie-breaking rules or randomization when multiple stable solutions exist can promote fairness and diversity in matches. Additionally, parallel computing and distributed systems can be employed to improve the algorithm's efficiency when dealing with large datasets, making it more adaptable and practical in real-world scenarios. These heuristics optimize the Gale-Shapley algorithm for various applications, ensuring better outcomes in matching problems.
